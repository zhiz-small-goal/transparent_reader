# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free, inclusive community for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Give and receive constructive feedback.
- Focus on solving problems and improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or disruptive behavior.
- Spam or off-topic content.

## Reporting
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it via project issues or contact maintainers privately.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal of contributions, to ensure a safe environment.
